Milk

Milk is one of the liquids that nearly every single person in the world drinks, and with good reason - it is extremely low in kilojoules (skim milk contains 187 kJ/100mL). It conatins a fairly high amount of calcium (300 milligrams per cup, or 120 mg/100g), and skim milk - one of the most commonly found varieties - contains less than 2% fat per 2 litre bottle (0.1g of fat per 100g of milk).

Milk costs roughly 30-50 cents per serve (cup), making it fairly affordable.

A slogan? Why yes:

More Inspiration, Less Kilojoules (MILK[j]).
